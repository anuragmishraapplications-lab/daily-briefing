US Opens Section 301 Trade Probes into India, China, and 14 Other Economies Over Industrial Overcapacity

The Office of the United States Trade Representative opened Section 301 investigations on 17 March against 16 economies — including India, China, the EU, Japan, South Korea, and Mexico — examining whether their manufacturing and industrial practices have generated excessive production capacity disconnected from genuine market demand.
